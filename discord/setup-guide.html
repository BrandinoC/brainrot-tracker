🧠 RotVault Discord Setup

Follow these steps in order. Click Copy on each box and paste into Discord.

1 Create your server

  1. Open Discord (app or browser)
  2. Click the + on the left → Create My Own
  3. Choose For a club or community
  4. Name it RotVault
  5. Upload server icon: rotvault-thumbnail.png in the brainrot-tracker folder
Open Discord

2 Create channels

Right-click your server → Create Category, then add these text channels. Full list:

INFO: #welcome #rules #announcements #download SUPPORT: #help #bug-reports #suggestions COMMUNITY: #general #show-your-haul #brainrot-talk Roles to add later: @Buyer (paid users)

3 Paste into #welcome

Welcome to **RotVault** 🧠 Track your Brainrot stock, sales, and profit for Steal The Brainrots. **Try free (browser demo)** https://brandinoc.github.io/brainrot-tracker/ **Full desktop app** Download from your Gumroad purchase email → RotVault Setup 1.0.0.exe **Need help?** → #help **Show off your hauls** → #show-your-haul Glad you're here!

4 Paste into #rules

**RotVault Server Rules** 1. **Be respectful** — no harassment or drama. 2. **Don't share the paid installer** — buyers only (Gumroad link). 3. **Use the right channel** — #help for support, not random DMs to staff. 4. **No spam** — no ads, scam links, or self-promo. 5. **Have fun** — we're here for Brainrots 🧠

5 Pin in #download

Paste this message, then right-click it → Pin Message

📥 **DOWNLOAD ROTVAULT** 🆓 **Free browser demo** https://brandinoc.github.io/brainrot-tracker/ 💎 **Full desktop app** (Gumroad buyers) Check your purchase email for **RotVault Setup 1.0.0.exe** ⚠️ Windows SmartScreen? Click *More info* → *Run anyway* ❓ Stuck? Ask in #help

6 Get your invite link

  1. Click your server name → Invite People
  2. Edit invite link → set to Never expire
  3. Copy the link (looks like https://discord.gg/xxxxx)
  4. Paste it into discord/YOUR-INVITE-LINK.txt
  5. Paste the same link in index.html → search for discord-invite

7 Gumroad buyer email (optional)

Add this to your Gumroad "content" or thank-you email. Replace the Discord line with your invite.
